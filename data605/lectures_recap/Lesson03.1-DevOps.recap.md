1. **Task-Formulation Question**: Describe the process of deploying an
   application using Docker containers. Include the roles of both developers and
   IT operations in this process and define key performance metrics that can be
   used to evaluate the success of such a deployment.

2. **Comparison Question**: Compare the container era with the virtual machine
   era in terms of resource efficiency and management overhead. In what
   scenarios would choosing one over the other be more advantageous,
   particularly in the context of a startup company with limited resources?

3. **Example-Based Question**: Consider a real-world application, such as an
   online banking system, that relies heavily on uptime and security. How does
   implementing Docker as part of the DevOps practice enhance the management and
   deployment of this application? Provide specific examples to justify your
   answer.

4. **Conceptual "How and Why" Question**: Explain how Docker's use of image
   layers contributes to the efficiency and speed of application deployment and
   updates. Discuss the practical significance of this mechanism in a
   continuously deployed environment.
