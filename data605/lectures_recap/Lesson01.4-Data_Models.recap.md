1. Discuss the importance of data modeling in the context of building scalable
   applications. What characteristics of a good data model contribute to its
   expressiveness, usability, and performance?

2. Compare and contrast the relational model and the object-oriented model of
   data representation. In what scenarios might one model be more advantageous
   than the other, considering factors such as data complexity and application
   requirements?

3. Explain how logical and physical data independence contribute to the
   efficiency of database systems. What implications do these types of
   independence have on the design and maintenance of large-scale data-driven
   applications?

4. Reflect on the evolution of data models from the hierarchical and network
   models to present-day NoSQL databases. What were the key motivations driving
   this evolution, and how have they shaped current data storage and retrieval
   practices?
