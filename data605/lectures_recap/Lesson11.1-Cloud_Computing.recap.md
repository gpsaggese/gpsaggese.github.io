1. Discuss the trade-offs between renting infrastructure (cloud computing) and
   buying infrastructure for an organization. What are the key performance
   metrics that should guide this decision, and how might these metrics differ
   based on the size and type of the organization?

2. Compare the three service models of cloud computing:
   Infrastructure-as-a-Service (IaaS), Platform-as-a-Service (PaaS), and
   Software-as-a-Service (SaaS). Provide specific examples of each service model
   and explain the advantages and disadvantages of each in terms of scalability,
   cost, and maintenance.

3. Reflect on the convergence of technologies that has made cloud computing
   viable in recent years. Which specific technological advancements do you
   consider most critical to this evolution, and why? Discuss how these
   advancements have impacted various industries.

4. Explain the concept of hybrid cloud deployments and provide scenarios where
   they are particularly beneficial. What considerations should organizations
   take into account when determining which workloads to keep on-premises versus
   migrating to the public cloud?
