## Revenue Models & Unit Economics

*
- For what value a Customer Segment is truly willing to pay?
- Revenue Streams can have different pricing mechanisms (e.g., fixed list price,
  bargaining, auctioning, volume dependent, market dependent)
- One-time payments vs ongoing payments

* Asset sale
- Sell ownership of a product

* Usage fee
- The more a service is used, the more the customer pays
- E.g., number of minutes spent on the phone
- E.g., a hotel charges by number of nights in a room

* Subscription fee
- Sell continuous access to a service
- E.g., monthly subscription to a gym or to a web-based game

* Lending / renting / leasing
- Temporarily granting rights to use an asset for a fixed period
- E.g., car rental

* Licensing
- Give customers permission to use intellectual property in exchange for a
  licensing fees
- E.g., patent / copyright in media industry and technology

* Brokerage fees
- Intermediation services performed on behalf of multiple parties
- E.g., credit card providers charge a percentage of transaction value
- E.g., real estate agents earn a commission when matching buyers and sellers

* Advertising
- Fees for advertising product, service, or brand
- E.g., media industry
